Privacy Policy — Stellar Rush Effective date: 17 May 2026 · Last updated: 17 May 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile game Stellar Rush ("the App") handles information when you use it. The App is developed and operated by Xitellar ("we", "us", "our"). Summary in plain English: Stellar Rush does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The App is fully offline. All game data stays on your device. 1. Information We Do Not Collect The App does not collect, request, transmit, or process any of the following: Your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifiers Your device's advertising ID, IMEI, MAC address, IP address, or other persistent identifiers Your location (precise or approximate) Your contacts, photos, files, microphone, or camera Account credentials of any kind — the App has no account system Analytics, crash reports, telemetry, or usage tracking Any data about other people you interact with 2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device The App stores the following game-state information in your device's local browser storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device, is never transmitted to us or any third party, and is automatically removed if you uninstall the App or clear its storage from Android's app settings: Your highest score and total stars earned Your settings preferences (volume levels, vibration, accessibility toggles) Skins and upgrades you have purchased using in-game stars Your daily mission progress and unlocked achievements Your local leaderboard (top 5 runs) Whether you have seen the in-game tutorial Your last login date (for the daily login bonus only) 3. Third-Party Services The App does not currently use any third-party services, advertising networks, analytics platforms, social-login providers, or external SDKs. If this ever changes in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated, the change will be disclosed in the App's update notes, and you will be informed in-app before any new behaviour takes effect. 4. Advertising The App displays no advertisements. 5. In-App Purchases The App has no real-money in-app purchases. All in-game items (skins, upgrades, consumables) are purchased with stars collected by playing. 6. Children's Privacy The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Because no information is collected at all, the App is fully compliant with COPPA and similar children's privacy regulations. 7. Permissions The App requests the following Android permissions: Vibration — used for short haptic feedback on impacts and pickups. You can disable vibration entirely in the in-app Settings menu at any time. The App does not request internet, location, storage, microphone, camera, contacts, or any other sensitive permission. 8. Data Security Because the App does not transmit any personal information, there is no data in transit that could be intercepted. Locally stored game data is protected by Android's standard app sandbox and your device's own security model. 9. Your Rights and Choices You may delete all locally stored data at any time by: Tapping Reset Best Score in the in-app Settings menu (clears high score only) Clearing the App's storage from Android's app settings (clears all game data) Uninstalling the App entirely (clears everything related to the App) 10. International Users Because the App does not collect or transmit personal data, no cross-border data transfers occur. The App is safe to use under GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and similar privacy regulations. 11. Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes to the App. If a future version begins to collect data, transmit data, or integrate third-party services, this page will be updated with a new "Last updated" date, and an in-App notice will inform you of the change before the new behaviour takes effect. 12.
